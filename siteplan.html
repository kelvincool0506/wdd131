CyberNest

Your personal guide to safer digital living.

Site Name

CyberNest — The name implies a secure space where users can learn to protect their online identity, data, and habits. It’s memorable, brandable, and aligned with digital safety.

Site Purpose

This site provides accessible guides and practical tips for everyday digital users, including tutorials on password management, scam avoidance, and privacy settings. It aims to empower users with knowledge and tools to secure their digital footprint.

Scenarios

  • How do I know if an email is a phishing attempt?
  • What steps should I take to protect my home Wi-Fi network?

Color Schema

  • #0b3c5d (Deep Blue) — Used for headings and primary buttons.
  • #d94f70 (Coral Pink) — Used as accent color for highlights and icons.
  • #eaf4f9 (Sky Blue) — Used as the page background for a clean and calming effect.

Typography

  • Montserrat — Headings, logo, call-to-action banners.
  • Open Sans — Body text, paragraphs, lists.
